# OBPushButton - Blue

## Introduction
---
 This is a digital push button sensor, which detects your press action and it is ideal for beginners to use.

 ![](./images/iM1rurh.jpg)

## Characteristics
---
- The 3-pins ports is easy to plug and play.
- It can work with micro:bit in 3V.

---
