## Hardware Connection

Connect a [motor](https://www.elecfreaks.com/geekservo-motor-2kg-compatible-with-lego.html) to M1 port on [Wukong breakout board](https://www.elecfreaks.com/wukong-board-with-lego-holder-for-micro-bit.html).
