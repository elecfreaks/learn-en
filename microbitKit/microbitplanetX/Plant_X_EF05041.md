# 18B20 Temperature Sensor 

## Introduction
It is a digital tempeterature sensor based on 1Maxim IC DS18B20 1-Wire.

![](./images/05041_01.png)

## Characteristic

---

- Designed in RJ11 connections, easy to plug.

## Specification

---

Item | Parameter 
:-: | :-: 
SKU|EF05041
Connection|RJ11
Type of Connection|Digital output
Working Voltage|3.3V
